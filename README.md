# Castle Sewer Complex +1 (sj3_chuma_pupluka.bsp)
Quake map for Alkaline (entry for Sewer jam 3)


TO PLAY: Drop the folder in your sourceport of preference... that's it... have fun! (preferrably in a folder with [Alkaline 1.2.4 devkit](https://github.com/fabiolimamp/alkaline/releases/tag/1.2.4))

### General Information / Behind the scenes.

(from Chuma's perspective)

This map happened to exist because i wanted to make a map that didn't push me to the boundaries as a mapper / Level designer and i do something for fun, just quick fun that doesn't take a lot of my time... something quick, fun and chumastic... Yes!

Altho it took a bit of crunch time because i started late as i finished a Doom mapping project and the jam was 2 weeks in... Never thought of anything big i wanted to do a sewer level with medieval theme/textures and i imported chotex textures to have some purple medieval castle textures.

Along the way Pup Luka wanted to do a mapping collab and i said yes, we couldn't manage to do a big collab map in this one due to constraints, hopefully in the future we do another collab for sure... But he agreed with my vision (became our vision) and he made sure the map was balanced, playable and fun.

The song for the map "Deadly Toxins" was me experimenting with a sample, as i've been experimenting with sampling in FL Studio and make a piano dark melody as a base... When i finished the song i didn't have any idea what to name it, the name was picked by my friend Virginia, in spirit i credit her the naming of the song.

Song uses a sample, a fragment of a song of an university of Chile (UACH), it's is Library closing song, the song of my band falls under fair use as it's intended for creative purposes and there shouldn't be any problem based in me and my bandmates investigation, leaving this information out and if there's any issue please reach out to me to solve out... I hope if everything it's considered as a practice of sampling.
